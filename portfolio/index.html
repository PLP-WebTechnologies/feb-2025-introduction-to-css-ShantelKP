highlights of what makes its vistas so special:

Cool Mountain views
Table Mountain: The flat-topped mountain offers panoramic views of the city, ocean, and surrounding peaks. The cable car ride or hike to the top is a must.

Constantia & Stellenbosch: Rolling vineyards with mountain backdrops, offering serene, lush landscapes.

City & Harbor Perspectives
Cape Town Stadium & Green Point: Modern architecture with sea and mountain views.
